The Greatest dish ever: Mtsvadi



Description

Mtsvadi is a traditional Georgia barbecue

It is massively enjoyed by me and many others


  • Good pork meat
  • Mayali
  • firewood
  • spices
  1. Season meat with salt and pepper
  2. light a fire using wood
  3. put the meat on mayali
  4. put the mayali over fire and constantly rotate it
